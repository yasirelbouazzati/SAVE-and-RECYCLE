The Old Man and the Clean Street



Mr. Thomas, a kind elderly man, found a messy street full of garbage while taking a walk.


He felt discouraged seeing the chaos.







However, he had a brilliant idea: to recycle!

He decided to start cleaning and recycling every object he found.

With effort and dedication, he transformed the street into a clean and orderly place.







Mr. Thomas felt happy to have helped the planet and turned the trash into something useful.





His example inspired other neighbors to keep the street clean and care for the environment.









THE END
